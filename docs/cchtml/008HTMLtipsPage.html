Peanut's Tips for Hiding the Perfect Nut

Storing a nut for later isn’t easy, but as every wise squirrel knows, You can always find a safe place for your nut.

Envisioning the Perfect Hiding Spot

Can you imagine what it would be like to have a nut hidden so well that no bird, raccoon, or forgetful future-you could ever find it?

—Peanut the Squirrel, How to Hide a Nut and Still Remember Where You Put It

Importance of Scouting the Area

So much of hiding a nut is knowing your surroundings.

It’s OK to be cautious, but you must leave the tree sometimes.

Check under leaves, near roots, and behind rocks.

Visit multiple trees and travel if you have to.

Importance of Misdirection

Pretend to bury your nut in one spot, then walk away.

Circle back when no one is watching.

Choose a place so ordinary that no other squirrel would suspect it.
