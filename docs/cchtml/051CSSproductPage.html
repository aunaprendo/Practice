Squirrel Training

Learning Outcomes

  • Demonstrate improved balance and coordination while navigating elevated branches, ropes, and narrow platforms.
  • Apply problem-solving skills to identify the safest and most efficient paths through complex obstacle sequences.
  • Exhibit increased strength and agility when leaping between varying distances and surfaces.
  • Develop spatial awareness to accurately judge heights, gaps, and landing zones.
  • Practice risk assessment by adapting movement strategies in response to unstable or moving obstacles.
  • Build endurance through sustained activity across multi-stage obstacle courses.
